5 HTML Tags I Learned

header
Represents the top section of a page or section (often contains title/logo/navigation).
nav
Contains the main navigation links for a website or a page.
main
Holds the main content of the page (unique content, not repeated across pages).
article
Represents a self-contained piece of content (like a blog post or profile section).
footer
Represents the bottom section of a page or section (copyright, links, contact info).
